Intro

Hi, I'm Nicole, a born and raised New Yorker, currently a Data Scientist at Ribbon Health working to simplify healthcare. Previously I was as Aetna and Uber. If you're curious about the details, check out my work page. I studied Business, with concentrations in Data Science and Statistics, at NYU Stern School of Business (undergrad).

In my free time I love to travel, read, and dance (salsa, latin, ballroom, hip hop). The background photo is of me and some friends diving in Chac-mool, a cenote in Cancun. I'm very passionate about doing things that allow me to connect with others in various ways.

Work

Data Scientist

July 2020 - March 2022

Launched and evaluated randomized outreach campaigns, known as Next Best Actions (NBAs) to encourage and inform members on how to better manage their chronic conditions, resulting in improved health outcomes and generating medical cost savings.

Data Analyst

June 2019 - August 2019

My intern project involved recommending driver segments to target for future expansion based on patterns identified in payment data from 1M+ drivers using logistic regression in R and UXR survey results.

Vice President of Insight Team & Project Manager

Supervised 4 teams of returning members in offering pro-bono data consulting services to tech startups.
